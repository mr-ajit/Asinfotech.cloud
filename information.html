Information availability

Information availability

In today’s big data environment uninterrupted access with unmatched performance to business critical information services 24X7X365 is more important than ever.

Information availability in enterprise environment is much more complex than it appears. Every system in the enterprise must be road mapped, its importance determined, and failsafe mechanisms addressed for every conceivable scenario.

ASINFOTECH offers a comprehensive suite of Storage, Disaster recovery, Business continuity and Archiving solutions for every need and budget.

Storage

Data storage is the core of IT infrastructure,organizations depend upon their ability to store, retrieve, manipulate, share, and manage data in a highly available, scalable, flexible and secure environment

Technologies like thin provisioning, automated storage tiering and de-duplication create innovative ways to get more value out of your storage investment. we specialize in designing feature rich storage solutions to get the best return from your storage investment.

From entry level to high-end enterprise class storage products. Tvishi offers a complete line of data storage solutions.

Backup and recovery

Backup and recovery: Data protection in enterprise is increasingly complex time-consuming and expensive with growing data volumes, virtualization, cloud computing and mobility. Tvishi understands these challenges, and can help customers architect and implement their backup and recovery platforms to meet today’s requirements for speed, efficiency and scalability.

Our enterprise-class backup and recovery solutions help shrink backup windows and enable centralized management of the data protection infrastructure. Tvishi also provide solutions that are optimized for virtualized environments, enabling faster backups and single-step restores while drastically improving server performance and reducing the amount of network bandwidth required for backup processes.

Business Continuity

IT service continuity undoubtedly is at or near the very top of every IT organization’s list of strategic initiatives, considering the dramatic costs and implications of downtime.

The best defense against disaster is well planned unified approach to recovery

ASINFOTECH can help organization to build solution which works for all business applications using technologies like Clustering, replication and virtualization based HA.
